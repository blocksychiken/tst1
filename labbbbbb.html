Gem Grab

Gem Grab is the first gamemode you unlock in brawlstars,the objective is to
get at least ten gems and have more gems than your enemies.If you die,you
lose all your gems .You get gems in Gem Grab by collecting them from the gem
mine.

> Showdown is the second gamemode you unlock in Brawlstars,Showdown is basiclly
